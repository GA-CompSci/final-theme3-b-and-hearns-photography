About

Hey, I'm Brendan. I’m a high school student at Gilmour Academy in Cleveland with a passion for photography. I specialize in capturing sports, portraits, events, and a variety of other moments through my lens. Whether it’s the fast-paced action of a game or the personality in a portrait, I enjoy telling stories through my photos. I’m always excited for new opportunities to shoot and would love to help anyone looking for photography services.
